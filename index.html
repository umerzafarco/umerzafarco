Frequently Asked Questions

How long will it take to develop my WordPress website?

Development timelines vary based on the project's complexity and specific requirements. A basic site may take a few days, while a custom site with advanced features could take a couple of weeks.

Will my WordPress website be mobile-friendly and responsive?

Yes, creating a responsive design that works seamlessly across all devices, including smartphones and tablets, is standard practice in modern WordPress development.

Can you customize my website to match my brand and unique requirements?

Absolutely! WordPress is highly customizable, allowing for themes, plugins, and custom code to fit your brand’s design and functionality needs.

What are your payment terms for WordPress development services?

My standard payment terms typically include an initial deposit to start the project, followed by milestone-based payments as development progresses, and a final payment upon project completion. I’m open to discussing flexible payment plans that suit both parties.

Will my website be SEO-friendly, and do you provide SEO optimization?

Yes, I develop WordPress websites with SEO best practices in mind, including proper site structure, clean code, and integration with SEO plugins to enhance search engine visibility.

What kind of security measures will you implement on my WordPress site?

I implement various security measures, such as installing security plugins, enabling SSL, ensuring regular updates, and configuring firewalls, to protect your site from vulnerabilities.

Can you develop a custom theme or plugin for my WordPress website?

Yes, I specialize in developing custom themes and plugins adapted to meet your specific requirements and functionality needs. This allows your website to have unique features that aren’t available with standard solutions.

Do you offer post-launch support and maintenance for my WordPress site?

Yes, I provide post-launch support that includes troubleshooting, updates, backups, and ongoing maintenance to keep your website running smoothly.

Can you migrate my website from another platform?

Yes, I can safely migrate your site from Wix, Squarespace, Shopify, or any other platform to WordPress with zero data loss and SEO preservation.

What makes your WordPress development unique?

I focus on performance, SEO, user experience, and scalability — blending technical precision with creative design to deliver websites that perform and convert.
